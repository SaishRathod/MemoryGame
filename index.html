Welcome to the Memory Game

Rules of the game:

  • The player first turns over 2 cards.
  • If the logos match, the player keeps the cards and tries again for another match.
  • If they do not match the cards are turned over again.
  • The player must try to remember where they have seen the cards, so they can use them to make a match.
  • The round ends when all of the cards have been matched.
  • Your scores will be calculated on the basis of time taken to complete the rounds.
